[￬ recorded-history.ttf](/fonts/recorded-history.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/recorded_history.png)

A low-resolution pixel font with as many straight lines as possible, designed to be easily readable at very small sizes.
Created 16 Jul 2021 to be used in [Touhou Spellbroken Dream Festival](/projects) for the spell history and bonus text.
Also hosted on [Fontstruct](https://fontstruct.com/fontstructions/show/1968214/recorded-history)

[￬ polariva.ttf](/fonts/polariva.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/polariva.png)

A trippy, distorted font created on an unknown date in 2021 using polar inversion

[￬ starprint-shard.ttf](/fonts/starprint-shard.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/starprint_shard.png)

A jagged, edgy font with small caps. It can be said to resemble barbed wire, insect legs, heavy metal band logos, tree
branches, thorns, crystals, jagged rocks, mysterious shards, or any number of things, giving it a wide variety of uses.
Created 30 Aug 2021. Also hosted on [Fontstruct](https://fontstruct.com/fontstructions/show/1984846/starprint-shard)

[￬ cyber-breakforce.ttf](/fonts/cyber-breakforce.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/cyber_breakforce.png)

A sci-fi font with an emphasis on 45-degree angles. Created 4 Sep 2021. Also hosted
on [Fontstruct](https://fontstruct.com/fontstructions/show/1987073/cyber-breakforce)

[￬ hallows-origin.ttf](/fonts/hallows-origin.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/hallows_origin.png)

A very tall, thin gothic font. It was made for Halloween, and will be perfect for spooky designs, but it'll work great
for non-seasonal designs as well. It could also be said to resemble nordic runes, or stereotypical "tribal" text.
Created 19 Oct 2021. Also hosted on [Fontstruct](https://fontstruct.com/fontstructions/show/2012111/hallows-origin)
