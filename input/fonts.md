[￬ recorded-history.ttf](/fonts/recorded-history.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/recorded_history.png)

A low-resolution pixel font with as many straight lines as possible, designed to be easily readable at very small sizes. Created 16 Jul 2021 to be used in [Touhou Spellbroken Dream Festival](/projects) for the spell history and bonus text

[￬ polariva.ttf](/fonts/polariva.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/polariva.png)

A trippy, distorted font created on an unknown date in 2021 using polar inversion

[￬ starprint-shard.ttf](/fonts/starprint-shard.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/starprint_shard.png)

A jagged, edgy font with small caps. It can be said to resemble barbed wire, insect legs, heavy metal band logos, tree branches, thorns, crystals, jagged rocks, mysterious shards, or any number of things, giving it a wide variety of uses

[￬ cyber-breakforce.ttf](/fonts/cyber-breakforce.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/cyber_breakforce.png)

A sci-fi font with an emphasis on 45-degree angles. Created 4 Sep 2021

[￬ hallows-origin.ttf](/fonts/hallows-origin.ttf)

![Characters of the font](/fonts/hallows_origin.png)

A very tall, thin gothic font. It was made for Halloween, and will be perfect for spooky designs, but it'll work great for non-seasonal designs as well. Created 19 Oct 2021
